History

The Maletsunyane Braai Festival began as a celebration of Basotho heritage and has grown into one of the most exciting tourism events in Lesotho.The festival started in the late 2010s as a way to celebrate Basotho culture, local food, and outdoor adventure.

Importance

The festival promotes local culture, supports tourism and brings together artists, musicians and visitors from around the world. Support local business, creates community engagement, and promotes outdoor adventure.

Who Should Attend

  • Tourists
  • Families
  • Students
  • Adventure travelers
